* Color pickers can help you find the color you want.
* It is important to ensure that there is enough contrast between any text in the background color ( otherwise people will not be able to read your content 
* CSS3 has introduce an extra value for RGB colors to indicate opacity. It is known as RGB a.
* CSS3 also allows you to specify colors as HSL values, with an optional opacity value. It is known as HSLA.
